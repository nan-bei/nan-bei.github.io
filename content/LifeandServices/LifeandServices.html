Introduction

Choosing to study in Kuala Lumpur is a happiness, the local cost of living and services is not high compared to other European and American category of countries, it is a very cost-effective place!

With the original intention of providing convenience and service guidance to every UM student, this website hopes that you can make your life more interesting by getting convenience on the website! It will provide a guide related to the most delicious food on campus, general shopping malls in the neighborhood, basic medical facilities, and exploring the local multicultural landscape from the aspect of life and services on campus. We hope it will be helpful to you, so go and explore!
